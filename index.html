Monday — Push · Chest · Shoulders · Triceps
Dumbbell Floor Press
4 sets · 8–10 reps
▾
How To Do It
  1. Lie on your back on the floor, a dumbbell in each hand at chest level, palms facing forward.
  2. Press both dumbbells straight up until arms are fully extended.
  3. Lower slowly until your upper arms touch the floor — pause briefly.
  4. Keep feet flat and core tight. Push back up with control.
🎽 Band Option
Anchor a resistance band under your upper back, hold one end in each hand at chest level, and press upward the same way.

Dumbbell Shoulder Press (Seated)
3 sets · 10–12 reps
▾
How To Do It
  1. Sit upright on a chair, dumbbell in each hand at shoulder height, palms facing forward.
  2. Press both dumbbells straight overhead until arms are fully extended.
  3. Lower back to shoulder height with control. Don't arch your lower back.
🎽 Band Option
Sit on the middle of the band, hold one end in each hand at shoulder height, and press overhead the same way.

Dumbbell Lateral Raise
3 sets · 12–15 reps
▾
How To Do It
  1. Stand tall, dumbbells at your sides, slight bend in elbows.
  2. Raise both arms out to the sides until they reach shoulder height.
  3. Pause one second at the top — don't shrug your shoulders.
  4. Lower slowly back to your sides.
🎽 Band Option
Stand on the middle of the band, hold one end in each hand, and raise your arms to the sides exactly as you would with dumbbells.

3 sets · 12 reps
▾
How To Do It

Tricep Overhead Extension
3 sets · 12–15 reps
▾
How To Do It
  1. Hold one dumbbell with both hands above your head, arms fully extended.
  2. Keep elbows pointing forward and close to your head — don't let them flare out.
  3. Slowly lower the dumbbell behind your head by bending at the elbows.
  4. Press back up. Only your forearms should move.
🎽 Band Option
Step on one end of the band, hold the other end behind your head with both hands, and extend upward the same way.

Diamond Push-Ups
2 sets · Max reps
▾
How To Do It
  1. Get into a push-up position. Place hands together under your chest — thumbs and index fingers touching to form a diamond shape.
  2. Lower your chest toward your hands, keeping elbows close to your body.
  3. Push back up. Keep your body in a straight line. Stop when your form breaks.
🎽 Band Option
Drape a resistance band across your upper back and hold one end under each palm as you do the diamond push-up.

Tuesday — Pull · Back · Biceps
Dumbbell Bent-Over Row
4 sets · 8–10 reps
▾
How To Do It
  1. Stand feet hip-width apart, a dumbbell in each hand.
  2. Hinge at the hips until your torso is roughly parallel to the floor. Keep your back flat.
  3. Pull both dumbbells up toward your lower ribs, squeezing your shoulder blades together.
  4. Lower with control. That's one rep.
🎽 Band Option
Stand on the middle of the band, hinge forward, and row both ends up toward your ribs the same way.

Single-Arm Dumbbell Row
3 sets · 10–12 reps each side
▾
How To Do It
  1. Place your left knee and left hand on a chair for support, right foot on the floor.

  4. Lower with control. Complete all reps, then switch sides.

▾
How To Do It

Wednesday — Legs + Glutes
Dumbbell Goblet Squat
4 sets · 10–12 reps
▾
How To Do It
  1. Hold one dumbbell vertically with both hands at your chest, cupped like a goblet.
  2. Stand with feet slightly wider than hip-width, toes turned out slightly.
  3. Push your hips back and bend your knees, lowering until thighs are parallel to the floor.
  4. Drive through your heels to stand back up. Keep your chest tall throughout.
🎽 Band Option
Place a loop band just above your knees. As you squat, push your knees outward against the band.

Dumbbell Romanian Deadlift
4 sets · 10–12 reps
▾
How To Do It
  1. Stand feet hip-width apart, dumbbells in front of your thighs, palms facing your legs.
  2. Push your hips back (not down) and lower the dumbbells along your legs, feeling the hamstring stretch.
  3. Keep your back flat and dumbbells close to your body the whole time.
  4. Drive your hips forward to stand. Stop lowering when your back wants to round.
🎽 Band Option
Stand on the band, hold both ends, and hinge exactly as described.

Thursday — Core + Conditioning
Dumbbell Woodchop
3 sets · 12 reps each side
▾
How To Do It
  1. Stand feet shoulder-width apart, holding one dumbbell with both hands above one shoulder.
  2. In one controlled diagonal motion, swing the dumbbell down and across toward the opposite hip.
  3. Rotate through your torso, not just your arms. Slowly reverse back up.
  4. Complete all reps on one side, then switch.
🎽 Band Option
Anchor the band low to one side of you. Hold both ends and perform the same diagonal chop upward across your body.

Plank
3 sets · 30–45 seconds
▾
How To Do It
  1. Get into a push-up position but rest on your forearms instead of hands.
  2. Keep your body in a perfectly straight line — hips neither sagging nor sticking up.
  3. Squeeze everything: abs, glutes, quads. Breathe steadily.
  4. If your hips drop, the set is over.
🎽 Band Option
Loop a band around your wrists and hold the plank position while slowly moving one arm forward at a time.

Dumbbell Russian Twist
3 sets · 20 reps total
▾
How To Do It
  1. Sit on the floor, knees bent, feet flat or slightly lifted. Hold one dumbbell with both hands at your chest.
  2. Lean back so your torso is at about 45 degrees.
  3. Rotate right, bringing the dumbbell beside your right hip. Then rotate left. That's two reps.
  4. Keep your core engaged — don't just swing your arms.

Dead Bug
3 sets · 10 reps each side
▾
How To Do It
  1. Lie on your back. Extend both arms toward the ceiling. Lift both legs so knees are above hips, shins parallel to the floor.

  4. Return to start, then repeat on the opposite side.

Friday — Full Body Power
Dumbbell Thruster
4 sets · 8–10 reps
▾
How To Do It
  1. Stand feet shoulder-width apart, a dumbbell in each hand at shoulder height, palms facing in.
  2. Squat down until your thighs are parallel to the floor.
  3. As you drive up out of the squat, press both dumbbells overhead in one fluid motion.
  4. Lower the dumbbells back to shoulders as you descend into the next squat. Squat + press = one rep.
🎽 Band Option
Stand on the band and hold both ends at shoulder height. Perform the exact same squat-to-press.

Push-Up to Renegade Row
4 sets · 6–8 reps each side
▾
How To Do It
  1. Start in a push-up position with a dumbbell in each hand on the floor.
  2. Do one push-up.
  3. At the top, shift your weight to your left hand and row the right dumbbell up to your hip.
  4. Lower it, then row the left side. That's one full rep. Keep your hips square to the floor.
🎽 Band Option
Drape a band across your upper back and hold one end under each palm during the push-up portion for added chest resistance.

Dumbbell Split Squat
3 sets · 10 reps each leg
▾
How To Do It
  1. Stand in a staggered stance — one foot forward, one back about 2–3 feet. Dumbbell in each hand.
  2. Lower your back knee toward the floor by bending both knees.
  3. Front shin stays vertical — knee over ankle, not shooting past your toes.
  4. Drive through your front heel to return to start. Complete all reps, then switch legs.
🎽 Band Option

Arnold Press
3 sets · 10–12 reps
▾
How To Do It
  1. Sit or stand with a dumbbell in each hand at shoulder height, palms facing YOU (toward your face).
  2. As you press overhead, rotate your wrists so that by the time arms are extended, palms face forward.
  3. Reverse the rotation as you lower — palms end up facing you again at the bottom.
  4. This rotation hits all three heads of the shoulder muscle.
🎽 Band Option
Sit on the middle of the band, hold one end in each hand, and perform the same rotation as you press overhead.

Hollow Body Hold
3 sets · 20–30 seconds
▾
How To Do It
  1. Lie on your back, arms extended overhead, legs straight out.
  2. Press your lower back firmly into the floor — zero gap.
  3. Simultaneously lift your arms, shoulders, and legs a few inches off the floor.
  4. Hold. If your lower back lifts off, bend your knees. Breathe steadily.
🎽 Band Option
Loop the band around your feet and hold the other end with both hands overhead while in the hollow body position. Advanced — master the standard version first.
